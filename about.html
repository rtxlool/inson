— Philosophy

We design houses for people who plan to stay. The work is residential, mostly — a quiet specialty practiced through four economic cycles and two architectural fashions.

Our rooms tend toward warmth: natural materials with some weight to them, lighting layered low, fewer objects than the photographs suggest. The first night should feel as settled as the thousandth. Nothing should ask for attention.

We work slowly on a small number of projects at a time, almost always in collaboration with the architect. We will turn down work that does not fit, and we have never tried to grow the studio past the point where Inson sees every drawing.
